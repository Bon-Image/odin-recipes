Side-up

This is very simple recipe that you can cook under 10 minutes and you can master just cooking it once!

Ingredients
  • Oil or butter (Prefered)
  • Fresh Eggs
  • Salt (optional)
  • pepper (optional)
Steps
  1. Heat the frying pan
  2. Melt the butte or heat the oil
  3. crack the eggs in the pan
  4. fry as you like it and serve.
