Steak

Start with taking the meat at least 30 minutes before cooking out of teh fridge. Seaon the meat with just salt, you can add pepper too, but nothing else.

After waiting an appropriate amount of time, melt the butter in a hot frying pan, as soon as the butter starts to bubble, put your meat in the pan and coat the non-frying side with the melted butter. Cook as you you like your meat. After you are done, let the meat stay in the pan for 5 to 10 minutes, then picked it up and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Fillet Pure
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Pepper (pptional)

Steps

  1. Season the meat
  2. Melt the butter to perfection
  3. Fry the meat
  4. Let it rest then enjoy
